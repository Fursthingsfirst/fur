ABOUT FURS THINGS FIRST

Who I Am

I run Furs Things First, focusing on practical, real-world dog training. My goal is to help owners build calm, well-behaved dogs through clear communication and consistency.

What I Do

I work with dogs of all ages, from puppies needing a solid start to dogs with behavioural issues like barking, pulling, or reactivity. Training is tailored to each dog and owner to get real results.

My Approach

I focus on simple, effective training methods that owners can actually follow day-to-day. No overcomplication—just clear structure, consistency, and results you can see.

About

Hi, I’m Adam — founder of Furs Things First based in Waterford, Ireland.

I started this business with the goal of helping dogs and their owners to live a happier, healthier life. Whether it’s training, walking, transport, or making sure your dog is part of your big day, I take pride in offering professional, personal and reliable care for every four-legged client.

With years of hands-on experience, full insurance, and a real passion for animals, I’ve built a service people trust — and dogs love. I understand that every pet is different, and I take the time to get to know each one, tailoring every service to suit their needs and personality.

Thank you for supporting us and I look forward to meeting you and your dog soon!

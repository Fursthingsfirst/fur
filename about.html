ABOUT FURS THINGS FIRST

Hi, I’m Adam Murphy, founder of Furs Things First. I’m originally from Waterford, and I’ve always had a strong connection with dogs. What started as a genuine interest in understanding their behaviour has grown into a professional dog training service built on trust, consistency, and real results. At Furs Things First, I specialise in helping owners build better relationships with their dogs through clear communication and practical training methods. Every dog is different, so I take a tailored approach to suit each dog’s personality, needs, and environment. Whether it’s basic obedience, problem behaviours, or improving overall control, my focus is on creating calm, well-balanced dogs and confident owners. I believe training should be straightforward, effective, and something that works in real life — not just in a training session. For me, this isn’t just a job — it’s something I genuinely care about. When you work with me, you’re getting honest guidance, hands-on support, and a commitment to helping you and your dog succeed.
